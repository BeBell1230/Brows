What it is

NanoBrows at Brows By Clari

NanoBrows use a single ultra-fine 'nano' needle and a digital machine to draw incredibly delicate, realistic hairstrokes. Because the strokes are machine-guided, nanobrows offer exceptional precision — and they're gentler on the skin, making them suitable for a wider range of skin types than traditional microblading.

It's the premium choice for clients who want hyper-natural brows with greater longevity and crisper healing.

Good to Know

NanoBrows FAQ

How are nanobrows different from microblading?

Both create hairstrokes, but nanobrows use a digital machine with a single ultra-fine needle instead of a hand blade. This is gentler on skin, often heals crisper, and suits more skin types.

How long do nanobrows last?

Generally 2 to 3 years with proper care, with a refresh to maintain crispness and color.

Is it more comfortable?

Many clients find the machine technique very comfortable, and numbing cream keeps the session easy.

Are nanobrows worth the premium?

If you want the most refined, natural hairstroke result with strong longevity — especially if microblading isn't ideal for your skin — nanobrows are absolutely worth it.
