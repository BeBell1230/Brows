What it is

Lip Blush at Brows By Clari

Lip blush is a semi-permanent tattoo technique that enhances the natural color and shape of your lips with a soft, buildable tint. It restores definition, evens out tone, can subtly correct asymmetry, and leaves lips looking healthier and more youthful — even with no lipstick on.

The finish is sheer and natural, not a bold lipstick line: think 'your lips but better,' with a fresh flush of color that lasts.

Good to Know

Lip Blush FAQ

Does lip blush make lips look fake?

No — lip blush is designed to look natural. It enhances your own lip color and shape rather than drawing a bold lipstick outline.

How long does lip blush last?

Typically 2 to 3 years depending on skin and lifestyle, with a refresh to keep color vibrant.

Does it hurt?

A strong topical numbing is used, so most clients are comfortable. Lips may feel a little dry or swollen for a day or two as they heal.

What does healing look like?

Color appears bright initially, then softens significantly over 5–7 days as the lips heal into a natural, even tint.
